Other apps that I use

03 Jan 2014

Hello everybody,

Yesterday, I'd share some apps that I use and today I'll cover other apps:

Alfred + Powerpack: If you're a Mac user and don't have Alfred installed yet, stop reading this, go ahead and download it right now. Seriously, Alfred has the power to boost your productivity, and Alfred + Powerpack has the power to REALLY boost your productivity. Seriously.

Dropbox + Google Drive: I've been using Dropbox for many years, really cool app, I can easily sync my files across my devices, and Google Drive too, the best feature about Google Drive (IMHO) it's the integration with all of other Google Products.

Adobe CC: Despite the fact that it's a little overpriced (IMHO), I really like this new Adobe's service.

Apple Pages: Lighter and simple than the Microsoft Word, Pages is my word processor of choice.

MAMP: I don't using this often like before, but when I have to use it, works very well.

Yep, I don't use any email client, so that's all.

Until the next time!
