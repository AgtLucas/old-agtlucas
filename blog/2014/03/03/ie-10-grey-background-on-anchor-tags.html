IE 10 Grey Background on anchors tag

03 Mar 2014

Maybe, if you use Normalize you never realised but, on IE 10 +, there's a weird behaviour on anchor tags.

When you click on a link/anchor tag without any background, IE 10 add a grey background, it's weird but it's easy to solve:

a:active {
  background-color: transparent;
}

And you're done. :)
